Find the answer hidden in an Italian greeting.

Clue 4: "In Italy, I’m a greeting said with flair; find me in cafés where locals share."

Think about a popular Italian greeting that can be found in cafés. This greeting is associated with the local culture and is often heard when friends meet.

Correct! The answer is "Ciao." Now you can proceed to the next clue.
